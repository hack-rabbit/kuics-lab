About
KUICSLAB is a security wargame for students to train their cybersecurity skills and knowledge.
How can I participate?
  • Challenges will be available after registration to this website.
  • In order to solve challenges, you have to submit contents of the flag file, usually at /home/xxx/flag.
  • Most challenges are running on Ubuntu 16.04 docker image.
Rules
  • Do not perform DoS(Denial of Service) attack on server, such as brute forcing.
  • If you found any unintended behavior or bugs, please report to admin.
